--- title: "Track Lap" excerpt: "Track Lap provides performance modelling of elite track sprinting
" collection: projects --- What is Track Lap?

Track Lap is a performance modelling system for elite track sprinting. Track lap uses split times to model the race and extract the following from 100m, 200m, and 400m races:

• Continuous velocity
• Peak velocity
• Time to peak velocity
• Distance to peak velocity
• Duration of the deceleraton phase
• Duration of the deceleraton phase
• Magnitude of acceleration
• Magnitude of the deceleration

We provide videos and race reports of races from which split times are validated.

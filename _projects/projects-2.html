" collection: projects --- What is Track Lap?

Track Lap uses race split times to model sprint race performance. Track Lap estimates the following data:

• Continuous velocity
• Peak velocity
• Time to peak velocity
• Distance to peak velocity
• Duration of the deceleraton phase
• Magnitude of acceleration
• Magnitude of deceleration

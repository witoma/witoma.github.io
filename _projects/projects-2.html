--- title: "Track Lap" excerpt: "Track Lap provides performance modelling of elite track sprinting
" collection: projects --- What is Track Lap?

Track Lap is a performance modelling system for elite track sprinting. Track lap can be used to assess the folling in 100m, 200m, and 400m races:

• Continuous velocity
• Peak velocity
• Time to peak velocity
• Distance to peak velocity
• Duration of the deceleraton phase
• Magnitude of the deceleration phase
